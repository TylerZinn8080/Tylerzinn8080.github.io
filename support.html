About CoreReset

CoreReset is an AI powered emotional regulation app that helps users stop reactive patterns and respond with calm, grounded clarity in real time.

When a text, comment, or boundary crossing triggers an emotional reaction, CoreReset analyzes what happened and generates a clear, practical response the user can apply immediately.

This is not journaling.
This is not therapy.
This is AI guided emotional clarity.

CoreReset is not a medical or mental health service and does not provide diagnosis or treatment.

How CoreReset Works

  • Describe what just happened in your own words
  • AI identifies the emotional trigger and reaction pattern
  • You receive a calm response aligned with self respect
  • Apply the aligned action and move forward with clarity

Designed for Everyday Emotional Situations

  • Stressful conversations and interpersonal conflict
  • People pleasing and boundary challenges
  • Overreacting to texts or online comments
  • Overthinking after social interactions
  • Losing clarity under pressure

CoreReset supports emotional regulation, decision making, boundary awareness, and self control without long sessions, coaching, or emotional digging.

The app includes a free trial. An account is required after the trial period to continue using CoreReset.
